Haoran Xu

Hello! I am now an algorithm engineer at JD Technology. I obtained a Master's degree from Xidian University, advised by Prof. Zheng Yu. My research topic lies in the field of Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning, especially offline (batch) RL and its applications. I am thrilled in building Artificial Intelligence that can learn purely from data and make reliable and effective decisions in real-world applications.

I previously interned at Microsoft Research Lab Asia (MSRA) during Summer 2018 and JD Technology from 2019.3 to 2021.6 on Urban Computing.
